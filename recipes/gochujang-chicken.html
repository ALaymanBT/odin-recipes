Gochujang Chicken

Description

Juicy, tender chicken bites in sweet, spicy, savoury gochujang sauce. What's not to like? This Korean-inspired spicy gochujang stir-fried chicken is easy, only requires few ingredients to make, and tastes amazing! And some word of advice, it's extra yummy and so so satisfying to eat with a bowl of warm, freshly cooked, steamy white rice. An easy favourite!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup gochujang
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, grated
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 2 lb. boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cubed
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons toasted sesame seeds
  • 3 tablespoons thinly sliced green onions
  • 6 cups cooked rice

Instructions

  1. Stir together gochujang, soy sauce, sweet rice wine, honey, garlic, sesame oil, and ginger in a small bowl.
  2. Heat oil over medium-high in a wok or large skillet. Add half of the chicken to the wok; cook and stir over medium-high until chicken is no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
  3. Remove chicken from the wok. Repeat with remaining chicken. Returnall cooked chicken pieces to the wok.
  4. Stir sauce well. Add to the wok; cook and stir until sauce has thickened and is bubbly, about 3 minutes.
  5. Top with sesame seeds and green onions. Serve immediately with rice.
